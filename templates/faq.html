Frequently Asked Questions

This a Hyper Local student Community Platform. This platform aims to cater queries from students who belong to the same college and particulary those queries which are highly relevant in the local setting but are vague and out of context when in a global setting.

This is a communinty of Students from your institution. Join your colleges community of students to learn and pool your knowledge and experiences to grow together along with building a valuable database for yourself and those to join the community in the future.

Any Kind of question or doubt you may have. As long as it is within community guidelines, you can ask any sort of question, be it a general doubt, regarding placements or about any event!

Simply Click the "Ask a question" Button. Give your question a title and a small decription of the problem or query you have and you can post it. You can Also add tags for easy searching.

Like Minded students from your college. Students who have experienced what you've asked or have gone through the same scenario, or someone who has information about your query would be answering you query.

Help regarding Placements, Interview Preparation, or any issue you might be facing. You can seek help regarding the placement process in your college or about the process of a certain company. You can also post your own interview experience and find the experiences of yours peers. Any information needed regarding events and functions can also be asked.
